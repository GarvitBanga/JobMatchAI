Privacy Policy for JobMatch

Last updated: January 2024

1. Information We Collect

JobMatch collects the following information:

  • Resume Data: When you upload your resume, we temporarily process it to extract skills, experience, and qualifications for job matching.
  • Job Data: We collect job posting information from career sites you visit to perform matching analysis.
  • Usage Data: Basic usage statistics to improve our service (no personal identification).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To match your resume against job postings
  • To provide relevance scores and analysis
  • To improve our AI matching algorithms
  • To provide customer support

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Resume data is processed temporarily and not stored permanently on our servers
  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS
  • We implement industry-standard security measures
  • You can delete your data at any time through the extension settings

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With your explicit consent

5. Third-Party Services

JobMatch uses AI services (OpenAI, Groq) to analyze job matches. These services process data according to their own privacy policies and do not retain your data.

6. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Opt-out of data processing

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes.
