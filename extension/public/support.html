Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload my resume?

Click the JobMatch extension icon, then click "Settings". In the settings page, you can upload your resume in PDF or DOCX format.

Which job sites are supported?

JobMatch works with most major job platforms including Workday, Greenhouse, Lever, BambooHR, and Amazon Jobs. We're constantly adding support for more platforms.

How accurate are the match scores?

Our AI-powered matching system provides scores based on skills, experience, and qualifications. While scores are a good indicator, we recommend reviewing the detailed analysis for each match.

Is my resume data secure?

Yes, we take security seriously. Your resume data is processed temporarily and not stored permanently. All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete your resume data at any time through the extension settings. Click the extension icon, go to Settings, and use the "Clear Data" option.
